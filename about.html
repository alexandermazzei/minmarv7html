About Minmar Windows and Doors

Expert window and door solutions in the Tri-State Area

Our Story

At Minmar Windows and Doors, we're dedicated to providing premium quality windows and door installations that enhance the beauty, comfort, and energy efficiency of homes across the Tri-State Area.

Founded with a commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction, we've grown into a trusted provider in the industry, while maintaining our core values and dedication to excellence.

Our expert installations and affordable pricing make us the go-to choice for homeowners looking to upgrade their windows and doors. We combine skilled craftsmanship with fast, efficient service to deliver solutions that exceed expectations for energy efficiency, durability, and performance.

Quality

We never compromise on materials or craftsmanship, ensuring that every installation we complete meets our rigorous standards.

Affordability

We offer premium windows and doors at wholesale prices, cutting out the middleman and passing the savings on to you.

Service

We're committed to providing exceptional customer service and fast, professional installation for all your window and door needs.

What We Offer

Expert Window Installation

We specialize in professional window installations that enhance your home's style and energy efficiency with precise workmanship.

Quality Door Solutions

Energy-Efficient Solutions

We offer energy-efficient window and door options that help lower utility bills and improve the comfort of your home year-round.

Fast, Professional Service

Comprehensive Support

Minmar provides full support from product selection through installation and beyond, ensuring complete customer satisfaction.
