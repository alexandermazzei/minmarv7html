Our Carbon Neutrality Pledge

We're on a mission to achieve carbon neutrality across all our operations by 2030. This commitment includes reducing emissions, investing in renewable energy, and offsetting remaining emissions through verified carbon offset programs.

  • 100% renewable energy in all manufacturing facilities by 2027
  • Zero waste to landfill across all operations by 2026
  • 50% reduction in supply chain emissions by 2028

Sustainable Product Features

Our windows and doors incorporate innovative technologies that make a real difference in energy efficiency and environmental impact.

High-Performance Glass

Our advanced low-emissivity (Low-E) glass coatings reflect infrared light, keeping heat inside in winter and outside in summer, reducing energy consumption year-round.

Recycled Materials

We use up to 40% post-consumer recycled content in our vinyl profiles, reducing waste and conserving natural resources without compromising quality or performance.

Longer Lifespan

Our products are built to last, with durable materials and construction that resist warping, fading, and deterioration, reducing the need for frequent replacements.

Energy Star Certified

All our products meet or exceed ENERGY STAR® certification requirements, ensuring they meet strict energy efficiency guidelines set by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.
