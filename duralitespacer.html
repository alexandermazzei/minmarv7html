DuraLite Spacer Technology

Advanced thermal barrier spacer system for superior window insulation and durability

What is DuraLite Spacer?

DuraLite is an advanced spacer system that separates glass panes in insulated glass units. Unlike traditional aluminum spacers, DuraLite features a composite structure with minimal metal content, dramatically reducing heat transfer at the edge of the glass while enhancing structural integrity and moisture protection.

Innovative Composite Design

DuraLite spacers use a unique no-metal design with a structural composite laminate and a desiccant-filled hollow core. This advanced construction eliminates the thermal bridge found in traditional metal spacers, significantly improving the window's overall energy efficiency.

The flexible nature of the DuraLite spacer system also accommodates natural expansion and contraction of the glass, reducing stress and ensuring long-term durability of the glass seal.

How DuraLite Spacers Work

Our advanced spacer system uses innovative materials and design to outperform traditional spacers.

The DuraLite system replaces traditional aluminum with a composite material structure that significantly reduces thermal conductivity. The spacer's hollow core contains desiccant materials that absorb moisture from within the sealed glass unit, preventing fog and condensation. The flexible nature of the composite material allows for expansion and contraction with temperature changes, maintaining the integrity of the seal and extending the lifespan of the window unit.
