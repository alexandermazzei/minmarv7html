UltraFlect 3 Triple Glass

Premium triple-pane glass for maximum energy efficiency and sound insulation

Superior Triple-Pane Technology

UltraFlect 3 represents the pinnacle of residential glass technology, featuring three panes of glass with two insulating chambers. This premium solution delivers exceptional thermal performance, substantially reducing heat transfer and providing superior sound insulation.

Each insulating chamber is filled with argon gas and separated by warm-edge spacers, while multiple Low-E coatings work together to reflect heat back to its source. The result is significantly improved energy efficiency and year-round comfort for your home.

How UltraFlect 3 is Built

Components:

  • 1.Three Panes of Glass: Creates two insulating air spaces for maximum thermal efficiency
  • 2.Double Low-E Coatings: Invisible metallic layers that reflect heat while allowing light
  • 3.Argon Gas Fill: Inert gas between panes that provides better insulation than air
  • 4.Warm-Edge Spacers: Non-metallic spacers that reduce heat transfer at the edge of the glass
