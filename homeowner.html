Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my windows?

We recommend cleaning your windows at least twice a year, typically in spring and fall. However, if you live in an area with high pollution, near saltwater, or experience frequent storms, more frequent cleaning may be necessary.

What's the best way to clean my vinyl windows?

Use a soft cloth or soft-bristle brush with a mixture of mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive cleaners, solvents, or sharp objects that could scratch or damage the vinyl. Rinse thoroughly with clean water and wipe dry with a soft, lint-free cloth.

How can I improve the operation of my sliding windows?

Keep the tracks clean and free of debris. Periodically vacuum the tracks and apply a silicone spray lubricant (never use oil-based lubricants on vinyl windows). For difficult operations, check that the window is properly aligned in the track.

What should I do about condensation on my windows?

Some condensation is normal, especially during seasonal changes. Excessive condensation may indicate high indoor humidity levels. Improve ventilation, use dehumidifiers in humid weather, or consider using ceiling fans to improve air circulation.

Are Minmar windows covered by warranty?

Yes, all Minmar windows and doors come with a comprehensive limited lifetime warranty. Coverage details vary by product line, so please refer to our warranty documentation for specifics.
