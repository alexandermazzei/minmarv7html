Key Features for Builders

Integrated Nailing Fin

Ensures secure attachment to the building structure and simplifies the installation process.

Multi-Chambered Frame Design

Provides superior insulation and structural integrity for long-term performance.

Pre-Punched Weep Holes

Facilitates proper drainage and prevents moisture accumulation.

Block & Tackle Balances

Ensures smooth operation and extends the operational life of operable windows.

Heavy-Duty Weather Stripping

Creates a tight seal against air and water infiltration in all conditions.

Fusion-Welded Corners

Enhances structural integrity and prevents leaks at frame joints.
