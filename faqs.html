Products

What types of windows does Minmar offer?

Minmar offers a complete line of premium vinyl windows including double hung, casement, slider, picture, bow, bay, and specialty shapes. Each style is available in multiple series to meet different performance needs and budgets.

Are Minmar windows energy efficient?

Yes, all Minmar windows are ENERGY STAR® certified and feature advanced energy-saving technologies such as Low-E glass, insulated frames, and warm-edge spacers. Our premium windows achieve U-Factors as low as 0.25, exceeding ENERGY STAR® requirements in all climate zones.

What materials are used in Minmar windows and doors?

Minmar windows feature premium vinyl frames that are durable, low-maintenance, and excellent insulators. Our doors are available in fiberglass, steel, and vinyl options, each engineered for optimal performance and longevity.

Can I customize the color and style of my windows and doors?

Absolutely! Minmar offers a wide range of customization options including exterior and interior color choices, decorative glass designs, grille patterns, hardware finishes, and more. Visit our Window Options page for complete details.

What is the difference between the various window series?

Minmar offers multiple series (9000, 8000, and 7000) that vary in terms of glass packages, frame design, hardware options, and energy performance. Higher series numbers generally indicate enhanced features and improved energy efficiency.

Installation & Measurement

Does Minmar provide installation services?

Minmar partners with a network of certified independent dealers who provide professional installation services. We can connect you with an authorized dealer in your area who has been trained in proper installation techniques.

How long does window installation typically take?

The installation time varies depending on the number and type of windows being installed, but typically replacement windows can be installed in one to two days for an average home. Your installer will provide a more specific timeline based on your project.

Warranties & Service

What warranty does Minmar offer on windows and doors?

Minmar offers a limited lifetime warranty on vinyl windows and patio doors, covering components such as frames, sashes, hardware, and insulated glass units. Entry doors come with a 10-year warranty. See our Warranty Information page for complete details and limitations.

Energy Efficiency

What does ENERGY STAR® certification mean?

ENERGY STAR® is a government-backed program that certifies products meeting strict energy efficiency guidelines. Minmar windows that carry the ENERGY STAR® label have been independently tested and verified to meet or exceed these standards, helping you save energy and reduce utility bills.
