TwinSeal Dual Glass

Advanced dual-pane glass system for superior energy performance

Advanced Dual-Pane Technology

TwinSeal Dual Glass combines two panes of glass with a sealed insulating chamber filled with argon gas. The outer pane features our advanced Low-E coating that works year-round to reflect heat while allowing natural light to pass through.

This high-performance system significantly improves your home's energy efficiency by reducing heat transfer, blocking harmful UV rays, and eliminating uncomfortable drafts. TwinSeal Dual Glass is our most popular option, delivering exceptional value and energy savings.

How TwinSeal Dual Glass Works

The Science Behind TwinSeal Dual Glass

  1. 1

    Low-E Coating

    Microscopically thin metallic coating on the glass reflects radiant heat back to its source while allowing visible light to pass through.

  2. 2

    Argon Gas Fill

    The space between glass panes is filled with argon gas, which insulates better than air to reduce heat transfer through conduction and convection.

  3. 3

    Warm-Edge Spacer

    Non-metallic spacer between glass panes reduces heat loss at the edge of the glass, virtually eliminating condensation and frost.
