Slider Windows

Contemporary style with smooth operation and maximum glass area

Minmar Slider Windows

Sliding windows easily glide open horizontally from the left or right, providing a wide view and full ventilation. With their sleek design and smooth horizontal sliding action, they're perfect for areas where projecting windows aren't practical. Choose from a 2-lite slider or a 3-lite slider window that features a large picture window with operable end vents.

2-Lite Slider

A two lite slider window will have two panes of glass; one which is stationary or "fixed" and the other pane moves side to side for easy operation. Ideal for areas with limited exterior space.

Get a Quote

3-Lite Slider

3-Lite slider windows have three panels (or sashes) within one master frame. Features a fixed center panel with two operating end panels that slide toward the middle. Perfect for wide openings with a picture window view.

4-Lite Slider

Our premium 4-lite slider windows provide maximum glass area with balanced visual appeal. Enjoy expansive views and flexible ventilation options with this advanced configuration.

Features & Benefits

Smooth Operation

All Minmar sliding windows have no roller tracks, allowing easy movement without the worry of roller failure.

Energy Efficiency

Multi-chamber vinyl frame design and weathersealing reduces energy loss.

Security

Heavy-duty cam-action locks provide enhanced security and peace of mind.

Easy Cleaning

Lift-out sashes for convenient cleaning of exterior glass from inside your home.

Space Saving

Ideal for areas where traditional swing-open windows would interfere with walkways or patios.

Low Maintenance

Durable vinyl construction never needs painting and won't rot, peel, or corrode.
