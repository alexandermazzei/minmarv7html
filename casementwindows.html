Casement Windows

Elegant design with maximum ventilation and unobstructed views

Minmar Casement Windows

Our casement windows combine elegant design with superior functionality. Hinged on the side and opening outward like a door, casement windows provide excellent ventilation and unobstructed views. The full-perimeter weatherstripping and multi-point locking system ensure exceptional energy efficiency and security.

Features & Benefits

  • ✓100% of the window opens for maximum ventilation
  • ✓Multi-point locking system for enhanced security
  • ✓Full-perimeter weatherstripping minimizes air infiltration
  • ✓Folding handle doesn't interfere with window treatments
  • ✓Advanced energy-efficient glass options
  • ✓Fusion-welded corners create a weathertight seal
  • ✓Smooth crank operation for easy opening and closing
  • ✓Available with various grid patterns and configurations
