Minmar Commits to Carbon Neutrality by 2030

May 15, 2025 • 6 min read

Today, we're proud to announce our commitment to achieving carbon neutrality across all our operations by 2030. This ambitious goal reflects our dedication to environmental stewardship and sustainable business practices.

Our Sustainability Roadmap

Our path to carbon neutrality includes a comprehensive strategy that addresses all aspects of our operations, from manufacturing to transportation and beyond. Here's how we plan to achieve this goal:

Renewable Energy

Transitioning to 100% renewable energy sources across all our facilities through solar panel installations and renewable energy credits.

Sustainable Materials

Increasing the use of recycled and sustainable materials in our products while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Energy Efficiency

Implementing cutting-edge energy efficiency measures in our manufacturing processes to reduce overall energy consumption.

Carbon Offsetting

Investing in verified carbon offset projects to neutralize any remaining emissions we can't eliminate through other measures.

Why This Matters

As a leader in the window and door industry, we recognize our responsibility to minimize our environmental impact. The building sector accounts for nearly 40% of global carbon emissions, and we're committed to being part of the solution.

"Sustainability isn't just good for the planet—it's good for business and our customers. Energy-efficient windows and doors are a critical component of sustainable building practices, and we're proud to lead the way in responsible manufacturing."
— Louie Laino, CEO of Minmar

Join Us on This Journey

We invite our customers, partners, and suppliers to join us in this important initiative. Together, we can make a significant impact on reducing carbon emissions and creating a more sustainable future.
